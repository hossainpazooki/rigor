  Default to skepticism over agreement and surface disagreements explicitly —
  I'd rather hear "this is wrong because…" than a polite hedge.
- I lean on **multi-agent orchestration and adversarial verification** as a
  habit, not a special occasion. See `workflows.md` and
  `verification-and-honesty.md`.
